About Us

"Don’t throw cash in the trash, recycle it all – it’s your call!"

OrganicBin is turning leftover organic garbage into materials that positivily impact the environment and economy.

As population and urbanisation grows, more food is being produced and more food is being wasted. Food waste is one of the least recoverd materials in the municiple solid waste stream and is one of the most impoertant materials to divert from landfills.

Our mission is not only to collect organic garbage from homes, institutions, restaurants etc. but we give price per KG in return to organic garbage so that public can earn from waste organic garbage and we also provide blogs for environment awareness.

We fertilize the waste garbage for farmers use, these will cut costs for farmers, restore soil health, and ensure proper waste management.
